It's the foundation for patient-
centered primary care.

Canvas creates a shared understanding of patient care across clinicians,
staff, and patients themselves - making you more productive, less stressed, and adding just a bit more joy into your practice of medicine.

It puts care at your command

You don't have to click a dozen times to order a flu shot or
bounce around five windows to discover the last blood
count. Canvas pulls everything you need directly into your
note, so you never have to leave it. And because Canvas
learns from every interaction, it gets smarter with every
patient you see.
